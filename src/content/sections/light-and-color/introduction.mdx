<div class="intro-title">Light, Color, and What We Actually See</div>
<div class="intro-sub">Part II — The physics of color — from wavelength to pigment, sky, and sea</div>

Color seems obvious. We open our eyes and we see it. But what color actually is — physically, at the level of light and matter — turns out to be something quite different from what we assume. This part lays out the basic science, which will become essential later.
