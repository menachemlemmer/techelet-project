<SubHead text="The Settled Premise" />

While much has been written regarding the identity of the chilazon, this work takes that identification as a settled premise. The confluence of circumstantial evidence and modern analytical testing is sufficiently overwhelming to treat the identity as a point of departure rather than a point of debate. This allows us to focus entirely on the nuances of the ancient practice itself.
